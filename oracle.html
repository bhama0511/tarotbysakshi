Asking the Right Questions

Safe & Helpful Questions
  • Clear & focused ("Will I get the job I applied for?")
  • About your own life & decisions ("What should I focus on right now?")
  • Open to guidance ("What do my angels want me to know?")
  • Timing-based ("Is the right time for this move?")
  • Positive or growth-focused ("What can I do to improve my relationship?")
Avoid These Questions
  • Too vague ("Tell me everything about my life")
  • Demanding exact names, dates, or places ("When exactly will I marry?")
  • About another person's private life ("Is my friend cheating on their partner?")
  • Medical, legal, or financial professional advice ("Should I stop my treatment?")
  • Life-and-death predictions ("When will I die?")
  • Repeatedly asking the same question for a different answer

Asking the Right Questions

Good Questions to Ask
  • Guidance-focused ("What animal spirit is guiding me right now?")
  • Energy insight ("What energy do I need to embrace at this time?")
  • Support & strength ("Which animal spirit can help me overcome my challenges?")
  • Self-growth ("What lesson is the animal spirit teaching me?")
  • Connection ("How can I connect more deeply with my spirit guide?")
  • Path clarity ("Which spirit animal is walking with me in this phase of life?")
Avoid These Questions
  • Exact predictions ("Which animal will bring me money next month?")
  • Life-and-death outcomes ("Will my pet survive?")
  • Medical/health questions ("What animal will cure my illness?")
  • Asking about someone else's animal spirit without consent
  • Repeatedly asking the same question hoping for a different card
  • Questions with no openness to guidance ("Which animal will make my ex come back immediately?")
