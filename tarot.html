Asking the Right Questions

Effective Questions
  • Open-ended questions ("What do I need to know about my career path?")
  • Questions focused on your own actions ("How can I improve my relationship?")
  • Questions about personal growth ("What energy should I embrace right now?")
  • Questions seeking guidance ("What should I be aware of in this situation?")
  • Questions about understanding ("Why am I feeling stuck in this area of my life?")
  • Questions about potential ("What opportunities should I look out for?")
Questions to Avoid
  • Yes/no questions ("Will I get the job?") - these limit the depth of insight
  • Questions about other people's private thoughts ("Does my ex still love me?")
  • Questions seeking exact timing ("When exactly will I meet my soulmate?")
  • Questions about health diagnoses ("Do I have a serious illness?")
  • Questions with predetermined answers ("Tell me I should quit my job")
  • Questions about death or harm ("When will someone die?")
